Introduction of Hockey and Hockey Club

Hockey is also known as ice hockey or ball hockey, which is a sport game between two teams of 11 players who use the stick to drive a small hard ball toward goals at opposite ends.

Sino Hockey club is a club that runs at 4:10-5:10 pm each Monday. You will play hockey and have some competitions with your friends.

People cannot kick the ball. Players are only allowed to hit the ball with the flat side of their stick except for the goalkeepers who are allowed to use their body or feet to prevent the opponent from scoring. (myactivesg.com.)

You should bring safety gloves, safety glasses, and a helmet to protect ourselves.

Why would you join this club? What are some expectations?

The expectations of the hockey club that is to have fun, meet new people(make new friends), and reduce our stress. The competition is the most inportant part that you can make new friends!
